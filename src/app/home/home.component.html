About

The Maine App Challenge is an opportunity for Maine high school students to create and present a mobile app of their own design for a chance to win one of three scholarships totaling $10,000. This site seeks to collect resources to guide students through the creation of their first Android and iOS applications so that they have the foundation necessary to craft high-quality submissions.

  • Learn more about the contest rules, submission process, prizes, and past winners at the official Maine App Challenge website.
  • Check out the FAQs for answers to common questions.
